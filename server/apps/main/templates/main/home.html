What Autism is

Autistic people often have different sensory processing to people who are not autistic .

By collecting together lots of autistic people’s experiences, we can change spaces so that they are better for autistic people.

How to Use the Platform

1. Register

an account on OpenHumans. When you register you create a private and anonymous account with Open Humans. We work with Open Humans to make sure you have control over your data and that it is safe.

2. Share

a story abbout sensory experiences. Enter a description of how your senses affect you. For example: “I do not go on trains because they are too loud”.

3. Choose

the viewing access of the stories. You can choose to share your story publicly or privately, for research. You can also share both publicly and with researchers if you prefer.

4. Check

shared stories. If you want to make your story public, it will first be checked to make sure it follows our code of conduct. This is to keep AutSPACEs an autism-first, inclusive and welcoming space for all.

5. Change

your stories any time. You can view your own stories and edit them, or change who they are shared with any time, even once they are submitted.
